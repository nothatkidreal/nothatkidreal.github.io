About NHK's Nextbots

All of the brainrot in one game! Escape as the relentless nextbots hunt you down, Run for your life from over 30 random pictures I found in my camera roll!

Installation

NHK's Nextbots was built in Minecraft and with the "MakeCode Agent", meaning you will require Minecraft Education Edition to play!, Download the world and the nxtbt file and copy the nxtbt file into your makecode agent as Javascript!
