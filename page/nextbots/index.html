About NHK's Nextbots

NHK's Nextbots is an adrenaline-pumping Minecraft game where relentless PNGs hunt you down. Your mission? Survive the pixelated onslaught and, of course, annoy your friends in the process.

The game combines the thrill of survival with the chaos of PNGs on the loose. Will you outwit them or become their pixelated prey?
